Introduction

Hi there, and thanks for taking the time to learn more about me! My career history includes experience in the diverse fields of Stage and Screen Performance and most recently, Web Development!

Outside of work you'll find me hosting delicious dinner parties for my family and friends, checking things off of my to-do list, or rewatching a classic film. If it involves creating, solving problems, or having fun, count me in!

Actor & Singer

I recieved my BFA in Musical Theatre from Kent State Univeristy where I honed my performance skills and gained a strong ability to connect with people. For the past 10 years I've had the opportunity to perform live across the Chicagoland area, and managed to make my way onto the small screen as well!

Being an artist is about making something out of nothing, and making an impact on someone's life, even if only for a moment.

Software Developer

After trying my hand with a few beginner coding tools, I was pleasantly surprised at how creative the coding process actually is! I was thrilled to discover a new and exciting outlet for my passion. My interest in the tech world and programming came to fruition upon my admission to the Actualize coding bootcamp.

Currently, I am a web developer with experience in Ruby on Rails and Javascript oriented frontend development. I am passionate about approaching this craft as an art, rather than a science and I believe I can continue being impactful combining my new skills with my training as an Actor!
