Actor

I recieved my BFA in Musical Theatre from Kent State Univeristy where I honed my performance skills and gained a strong ability to connect with people. For the past 10 years I've had the opportunity to perform live across the Chicagoland area, and managed to make my way onto the small screen as well!

Being an artist is about making something out of nothing, telling a story and making an impact on someone's life, even if only for a moment.

Software Developer

Coding is super creative, and I am thrilled that I've found another passion for my artistic energy. My interest in programming came to fruition upon my admission to the Actualize coding bootcamp. I am proud to say I graduated the program in March after building a fullstack web app called Dayly! Through Dayly, a user can create home light automation by way of a daily events scheduler. This app helps a user organize their schedule, while cutting costs on their electric bill. The backend of this application uses Ruby on Rails, a Javascript oriented frontend and integrates the Wink API and Yahoo Weather API.

Currently, I am looking to join a development team that values mentorship, innovation and is passionate about being helpful to the world. I am passionate about approaching this craft as an art, rather than a science and I believe I can be of great value combining my new skills with my training as an Actor!
